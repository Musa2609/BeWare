## 📌 Overview

**BeWare** is a comprehensive digital privacy platform that helps users understand, visualize, and control their digital footprint. It detects email breaches, analyzes ad manipulation tactics, and provides 10+ privacy tools - all in one beautiful dashboard.

## ✨ Features

### Core Features
- 🔐 **User Authentication** - Secure JWT-based login/register
- 📧 **Email Breach Scanner** - Check if your email appears in data breaches
- 🎯 **Ad Manipulation Detector** - Identify psychological tactics in advertisements
- 📊 **Privacy Risk Meter** - Visual gauge showing 0-100% risk score
- 📋 **Copy to Clipboard** - One-click report copying
- 📄 **PDF Report Export** - Download professional privacy reports

### 10 Privacy Tools
1. 🔐 **Password Strength Checker** - Rate your password security
2. 🗑️ **Data Broker Opt-Out Guide** - Remove yourself from data brokers
3. 🛡️ **Tracker Blocker Status** - Simulated ad/tracker blocking
4. 🖥️ **Browser Fingerprint Check** - See what your browser reveals
5. 🌑 **Dark Web Monitor** - Simulated dark web scanning
6. 📈 **Privacy History Chart** - Track your privacy score over time
7. 📱 **Social Media Privacy Check** - Audit your social accounts
8. 🔒 **VPN Recommendation Engine** - Get personalized VPN suggestions
9. 🍪 **Cookie Scanner** - View cookies stored by websites
10. 💡 **Privacy Tips Widget** - Rotating privacy advice
